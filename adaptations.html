Pride & Prejudice (1967)

This 1960s adaptation of ride & Prejudice was released as a miniseries in 1967 on BBC for the 150th anniversary of Jane Austen's death. This miniseries is known to be

the earliest BBC TV adaptation, due to the earlier BBC adaptations from 1938 and 1952 being considered "lost".

Pride & Prejudice (1995)

Another of BBC's Pride and Prejudice adaptations released as a miniseries as well. It remains true to the novel. Many fans of Austen's work agree that this is the most

historically accurate adaptation and some may even argue that it is the best one yet.
